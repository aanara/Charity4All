Donate Now!

Step 1: Choose your Item

Enter "Donate" and the name of the item you wish to donate where it says "I want to...". If you have no specific item in mind, type "Donate Clothes".

Step 2: Input Your Location

Enter your address, city, or zip code where indicated

Step 3: Results

Click search to view results! On the side of the map you will see 20 charities in your area. Below the map, you will see a list of charities within a 10 minute drive of your location. You can click on the name of a charity to get more information!

Note: Places where you can also purchase items will be marked with a bag icon, while places you can only donate will be marked with a square.
